This website contains rankings for Computer Science Conferences and Universities. The rankings are calculated using a HITS-like algorithm. The venue ranking is based on the number of good universities publishing at the venue, and the university ranking is based at the number of good venues it has published. These rankings should not be seen as general rankings, merely rankings that show the best universities in terms of research output at (top) computer science conferences. For more info on the ranking, please see 'About the ranking method'. This website presents the top 1000 universities. For each university, click on the arrow next to the name to see at which conferences they have published.
